## Context

What is the problem? What forces are at play (technical, organisational,
political, budget)? Keep this factual. A reader six months from now should
be able to reconstruct what the world looked like when the decision was made.

## Decision

What did we decide to do? One or two sentences of the actual choice, no
waffle.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or impossible as a result of this decision?
Include the *bad* consequences. The point is honesty: every decision costs
something.

## Alternatives considered

List each alternative with one paragraph: what was attractive about it, and
why we rejected it. "We didn't think of X" is also a valid entry (and a
signal to future maintainers that X might be worth a look).

## References

Links to PRs, issues, prior art, external docs that informed the decision.
